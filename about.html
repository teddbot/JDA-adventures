Our Vision

To connect people to life-enriching travel experiences. Jurgis, Dinah & Asenno witnessed this first-hand when they began going on tours. They saw the power that travel has bringing people together from varying backgrounds, cultures, countries and religions, and sought to give people across the globe the same great experiences that they had.

Our Mission

Offer a personalized service of quality and reliability through careful attention to properly training our personnel, designing unique journeys at affordable prices, and exceeding the expectations of our clients. On the other hand, we have the mission to maintain principles of sustainability and responsible tourism in the destinations in which we operate, driving experiences to an elevated level of satisfaction for the travelers, which constantly propels us to achieve better efficiency in our services.

Our Company story

JDA Adventures company history dates back in 2018, when two of the company founders met at a hike adventure on Mt.Kilimanjaro.

Jurģis Dzērve a tourist from North Eastern Europe and Antony Asenno a guide from Tanzania sparked a great connection during the trip and stayed in touch even after the trip was over which led to the beginning of a good friendship.

A year later, Jurģis was on a trip in Kenya and met up with Asenno and that's when the idea of founding a tours adventure company was born.

With knowledge of the European business ethic standard and more than seven years of tourism experience in Tanzania they both thought of brainstorming further,bringing whatever knowledge they had to the table with the intention of making this dream a reality.

Later on both Jurģis and Asenno decided to introduce a third party Dinah Karia from Kenya whose experience is in hospitality and also brings in her knowledge and skills in customer service and Web development.

Together the trio has worked tirelessly to be able to provide enjoyable and quality trips in and around East Africa on time and on a budget with world class customer service.

We're turning your travel dreams into reality by making those dreams fun and attainable, we help travelers expand their horizons, clear their heads and open their hearts for beautiful nature of East Africa Countries.

Our purpose is to give you exquisite tour experiences that will create pleasurable memories for you and your loved ones.
